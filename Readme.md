- HR Analytics Dashboard — Power BI

- Project Overview
The "HR Analytics Dashboard" provides an in-depth analysis of employee data such as "attrition", "performance", "salary" and "department".  
It helps HR teams take data-driven actions to improve employee retention, satisfaction, and workforce planning.

---

- Purpose of the Dashboard
- Identify factors influencing employee attrition    
- Understand retention trends  
- Measure performance distribution  
- Support HR planning & workforce optimization  

---

- ✔ Visualizations  
- Attrition by Department  
- Attrition by Age Group  
- Attrition by Job Role  
- Attrition by Gender  
  

- ✔ Slicers 
- Department  
- Job Role  
- Education Level

- "Power BI Desktop"
